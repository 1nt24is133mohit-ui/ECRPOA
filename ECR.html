Empowering Promotee Officers in East Central Railway

About Us

The East Central Railway Promotee Officers Association is dedicated to supporting and advocating for promotee officers within the East Central Railway zone. Our mission is to foster professional development, address concerns, and promote unity among our members.

Objectives

  • Provide a platform for networking and collaboration.
  • Advocate for fair policies and promotions.
  • Organize workshops and seminars for skill enhancement.
  • Support welfare initiatives for members and their families.

Contact Us

For inquiries, please reach out to our secretary at secretary@ecrpoa.org or visit our office at East Central Railway Headquarters, Hajipur, Bihar.
